# Hiller Aviation Museum

## Summary

Hiller Aviation Museum in San Carlos is an aviation museum at San Carlos Airport with aircraft displays, cockpit and simulator experiences, and family-friendly hands-on activities.

## Visit Facts

- Type: point_of_attraction / museum
- Address: 601 Skyway Rd., San Carlos, CA 94070
- Hours: open daily 10 am-5 pm; closed Easter Sunday, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.
- Estimated visit duration: 1.5-3 hours
- Family notes: children 3 and under are free with paid adult; free parking; wheelchair accessible; several activities are suitable for young children.

## Point Of Attraction Notes

- Key points of interest: aircraft on display, Boeing 747-100 nose section, flight simulators, Imagination Playground and launch activities.
