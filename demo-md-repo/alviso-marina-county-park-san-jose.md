# Alviso Marina County Park

## Summary

Alviso Marina County Park is a Santa Clara County baylands park in Alviso with pathways, boardwalks, picnic areas, marsh views, and boating access.

## Visit Facts

- Address: 1195 Hope Street, San Jose, CA 95002
- Vehicle entry: year-round 8:00 AM-sunset
- Trail hours: sunrise-sunset
- Fees: no fees collected
- Access risk: dogs, drones, and walking near the pond edge are prohibited; after-hours boat ramp access requires a gate key

## Point Of Attraction Notes

- Category: park
- Key points of interest: boardwalks and pathways, boat launch ramp, San Francisco Bay Trail / Bay Area Water Trail access, Pond A12 pink salt pond
- Best use: short bayland walk, picnic, birding, or boating launch point
