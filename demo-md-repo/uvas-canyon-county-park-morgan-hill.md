# Uvas Canyon County Park

## Summary

Uvas Canyon County Park is a Santa Clara County park near Morgan Hill with wooded canyon trails, waterfalls, camping, and picnic areas.

## Visit Facts

- Address: 8515 Croy Road, Morgan Hill, CA 95037
- Vehicle entry: year-round 8:00 AM-sunset
- Trail hours: sunrise-sunset
- Fees: vehicle entry fees collected year-round
- Reservation risk: vehicle and parking reservations are required seven days a week
- Pet restriction: no pets on trails; dogs allowed only in campgrounds

## Point Of Attraction Notes

- Category: park / county park
- Key points of interest: Waterfall Loop Trail, Black Rock Falls, Swanson Creek, campground and picnic areas
- Best use: waterfall hike and picnic plan, with reservations handled before departure
